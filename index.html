About Me

I'm Luis, a passionate data analytics enthusiast with a background that started as a hobby back in 2012. It was then that I began programming, my journey initiated with Lua on Roblox.

Fast forward to today, and I find myself deciphering the ever-evolving landscape of virtual reality (VR) with the help of Unity. My AS degree in Computer Programming and Analysis laid the groundwork for my technical skills, which I now leverage to create immersive VR experiences and explore the limitless possibilities of this exciting space.

As a data enthusiast, I see data as more than just numbers; it's a way to tell stories and make informed decisions. Whether I'm crunching data or attemting to craft VR worlds, my goal remains the same: to blend creativity and technology to bring ideas to life.

Outside the digital realm, I enjoy building custom computers and managing my own homelab, including my Unraid server. These hands-on pursuits provide the perfect balance to my digital adventures.

Join me on my journey as I continue to dive deeper into the VR world, decode data narratives, and explore the endless possibilities that technology and creativity offer.
